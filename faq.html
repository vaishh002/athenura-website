Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our internship program, roles and benefits.

Internships at Athenura are unpaid and do not include any salary, wages, or monetary stipend. However, we provide significant non‑monetary benefits including structured training, mentorship, and real‑world project exposure designed to enhance practical skills and employability.

Successful completion of the internship does not guarantee permanent employment or job placement. Any future employment opportunities are separate from the internship program and will only be communicated formally in writing by the company if applicable.

Yes. An Internship Completion Certificate is issued upon successful completion of the full internship duration, subject to satisfactory performance, project submission, and compliance with all company policies. Failure to meet these conditions may result in the certificate not being issued.

Yes. Internships may be offered in a remote format depending on project requirements, departmental needs, and company policies in effect at the time of onboarding.

The internship duration is specified in the official offer letter issued to the selected candidate. Duration may vary based on role and program structure. Extensions or early terminations remain at the sole discretion of Athenura management.

Internship opportunities at Athenura are open to students currently enrolled in recognized educational institutions as well as fresh graduates seeking practical industry exposure and skill development.

Selection is based on multiple evaluation factors including academic background, relevant technical or professional skills, demonstrated interest in the domain, and current project requirements. Athenura reserves the right to accept or reject any application at its discretion.

Yes. Each intern may be assigned a mentor or reporting supervisor responsible for providing guidance, monitoring progress, assigning tasks, and offering performance feedback throughout the internship period.

Interns are expected to: • Complete assigned tasks within deadlines • Maintain professional communication • Follow company policies and reporting structures • Participate in meetings and reviews • Deliver project work with quality and accountability

Interns may request early termination by submitting a formal written notice stating the reason. Approval is subject to management discretion and completion of all exit formalities, including task handover where applicable.

No. Athenura does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioral tracking technologies. Users can browse the website without cookies being placed on their device.

We collect only the information voluntarily provided by applicants or users, such as name, email address, contact number, educational details, and resume documents, strictly for application processing or responding to inquiries.

Athenura does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. Data may only be shared if required by law or for internship verification purposes with the applicant’s explicit consent.

We implement encrypted systems, restricted access controls, and secure storage protocols to safeguard user data. While we follow industry‑standard security practices, no electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.
