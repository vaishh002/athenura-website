Introduction

At Athenura, your privacy is our top priority. We believe in transparency and want you to understand how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information we receive from users of our platform and services.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We regularly review and update our privacy practices to ensure they meet the highest standards of data protection.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our service to you:

Usage Data

How We Use Your Data

Athenura uses the collected data for various purposes, all aimed at enhancing your experience and maintaining our service quality:

  1. 01.
    To provide and maintain our Service

    Ensuring the platform functions smoothly and handling user support requests effectively.

  2. 02.

  3. 03.
    To provide customer care and support

Data Security

"The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure."

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256), secure socket layer (SSL) technology, and multi-factor authentication (MFA) to protect your information. Our security protocols are audited annually by independent third-party experts.
